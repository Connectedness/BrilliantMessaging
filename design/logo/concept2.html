Concept 2 — The Controlled Flow

The idea

A single continuous trace, drawn like a printed-circuit path. The message enters at the left pad, is routed through a structured junction — the diamond exchange — via clean right-angle bends, and exits at the right pad.

Nothing wanders; every turn is deliberate. The aesthetic is intentionally technical and minimal — closer to Stripe / Linear / Vercel than to a speech bubble — which fits BMF's promise of messaging you can actually reason about.

The mark

Compact icon

The wide trace folds into a square for favicons and app icons — same enter / route / exit story, stacked diagonally.
