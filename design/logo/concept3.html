Concept 3 — The Pulse

The idea

A central exchange fans out to three endpoint nodes. The three connecting arms all bow the same way, so the mark reads as caught mid-spin: a living topology rather than a static diagram.

The teal dot is a message in flight, routed out to a subscriber — the pulse the concept is named for. One exchange, many endpoints: the fan-out shape of messaging itself. (The hero above is animated — watch the messages flow.)

Hub shape

Variant 1 · Diamond

The original — a square on its corner. Its 4-fold symmetry can't line up evenly with three arms, so the connections differ slightly; kept here as the baseline.

Variant 2 · Reuleaux triangle

A curved-sided triangle. Its three points aim straight down the arms and its curves rhyme with them — harmonious, organic, and it looks like it's mid-roll.

Variant 3 · Hexagon

Reads as a packet / event — on-brand for a CloudEvents-first framework. Its six sides contain the three-fold symmetry, so every arm connects identically.
